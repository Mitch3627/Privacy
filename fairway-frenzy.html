Fairway Frenzy — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 3 July 2026 · App package: com.ameb.fairwayfrenzy · Publisher: AMEB Studio

Summary

Fairway Frenzy is a casual offline golf-maze game. We do not ask for an account, we do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or files, and the game is fully playable offline. The only data that leaves your device is the advertising data described below, handled by Google AdMob.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The game shows advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and share your device's Advertising ID and coarse technical data (device model, IP address, app package name, ad interaction events) to serve and measure ads, including personalised ads where permitted. This data is collected and shared with Google and its certified ad partners. You can reset or delete your Advertising ID and opt out of ad personalisation at any time in Android Settings ▸ Privacy ▸ Ads. See Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data in AdMob apps.

Purchases

Optional in-app purchases (remove ads, undo packs, coin packs) are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details.

Game progress

Your progress (levels completed, coins, scores, settings) is saved only in a local file on your device. It is not uploaded anywhere and is deleted when you uninstall the app. It is not "collected" by us.

Notifications

The game may schedule local reminder notifications on your device. These are generated on-device; no data is transmitted.

Children

The game is rated Everyone but is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children; the only third-party processing is the AdMob advertising described above. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, contact us and we will assist.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all locally saved progress. To exercise data rights regarding advertising data, use the Google links above.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
