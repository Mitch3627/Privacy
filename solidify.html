Solidify — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 4 July 2026

Solidify ("the app") is published by AMEB Studio ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data we collect

The app itself does not require an account and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, or contacts.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app shows advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and share your device's Advertising ID and related signals (approximate location derived from IP, device model, ad interaction data) to serve and measure ads. This data is used for advertising and analytics purposes and is encrypted in transit. You can reset or delete your Advertising ID in your device's Google settings, and opt out of ad personalisation. See Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob data usage.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details.

Game progress

Your progress (levels, coins, settings) is saved only on your device. It is never uploaded to us. Uninstalling the app deletes it.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. It targets a general audience (rated Everyone). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children; ads are served as non-child-directed.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
