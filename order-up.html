Privacy Policy — Order Up!

Order Up! Food Sort Puzzle · package com.ameb.orderup
Last updated: 16 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the mobile game Order Up! ("the Game", "we", "us") handles, why, and who it is shared with. By installing or playing Order Up! you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

Short version: Order Up! has no accounts and no login. Your game progress is saved only on your device. The Game shows ads and uses analytics, and for those purposes a mobile Advertising ID is collected and shared with our advertising and analytics partners. We never ask for your name, email, or any personal contact details.

1. Information we collect

Order Up! does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to provide any personally identifying information. The Game and its third-party service providers may collect the following automatically:

DataPurposeShared?
Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID) Serving and measuring ads, limiting repeated ads, and analytics Yes, shared with our advertising and analytics partners
Approximate app-usage & event data (e.g. levels played, sessions, ad interactions, crash diagnostics) Understanding how the Game is used and improving it Yes, processed by our analytics partner
Device & technical data (device model, OS version, language, coarse region, network state) Ad delivery, crash reporting, and compatibility Yes, with advertising/analytics partners

Your game progress, settings, coins, Tokens and purchases are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your saved game to our servers.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Order Up! displays advertising through Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may use your Advertising ID and related device signals to deliver and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted. You can reset or delete your Advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization, at any time in your device settings (Settings › Google › Ads on Android).

  • Google's advertising practices: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • How Google uses data from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. Analytics (Firebase / Google Analytics)

Order Up! uses Firebase Analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase) to understand gameplay in aggregate, such as which levels are played and how the Game performs, so we can improve it. This data is tied to the Advertising ID and app-instance identifiers, not to your real-world identity. See Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Purchases

Order Up! offers optional in-app purchases (for example, removing ads, coins and Tokens). Payments are processed by Google Play. We never see or store your card or payment details. Those are handled entirely by Google Play under Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Notifications

Order Up! may schedule optional reminder notifications (for example, to let you know your daily reward is ready). These are local notifications: they are created and delivered entirely on your device, and no data is sent to us or to any third party to produce them. You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings (Settings › Apps › Order Up! › Notifications on Android).

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data described above is shared only with the service providers that make the Game work, principally Google (AdMob, Firebase Analytics and Google Play), for advertising, analytics and payment processing. It may also be disclosed if required by law.

7. Data retention

Locally stored game data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the Game. Data held by our advertising and analytics partners is retained according to their own policies. You can clear local data by uninstalling Order Up! or clearing the app's storage.

8. Children's privacy

Order Up! is a general-audience game and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The Game is not enrolled in Google Play's "Designed for Families" program. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

9. Your choices

  • Reset or delete your Advertising ID, or opt out of personalized ads, in your device settings.
  • Purchase "Remove Ads" in the Game to stop banner and interstitial advertising.
  • Turn off Order Up! notifications in your device settings.
  • Uninstall the Game to remove all locally stored data.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, within the Game.
