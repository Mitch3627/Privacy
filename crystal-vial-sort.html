Privacy Policy — Crystal Vial Sort

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Crystal Vial Sort ("the Game", "we", "us") handles information when you play. By playing the Game you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

The Game does not require an account and does not collect personally identifying information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

The Game uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. To serve ads, AdMob and its partners may collect and process your device's Advertising ID and related technical data (device type, OS version, coarse/approximate location derived from IP, and ad-interaction data). This data is used to deliver and measure ads and, where permitted, to personalise them.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To display and measure advertising (Google AdMob).
  • To process in-app purchases (Google Play Billing).
  • To improve gameplay balance through aggregated, non-identifying analytics.

On-Device Save Data

Your progress, coins, hints, settings and discovered crystals are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to us. Uninstalling the Game removes this data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. The Advertising ID and related technical data are shared with Google AdMob and its advertising partners solely to provide advertising. See Google's policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

The Game is rated for everyone but is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. The Game is not enrolled in Google Play's Designed for Families programme and serves standard (non-child-directed) ads. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will assist.

Your Choices

You can reset or limit ad personalisation at any time via your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads), and you can purchase "Remove Ads" within the Game to stop advertising.
